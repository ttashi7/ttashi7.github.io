Leadership Memphis Case Study

CRM Data Quality & Workflow Automation

Improving constituent data quality and developing a more efficient process for contact enrichment, outreach preparation, and organizational reporting.

Data Analytics CRM Data Quality Workflow Automation Business Intelligence

Organization

Leadership Memphis

A nonprofit organization supporting leadership development and community engagement.

My Role

Data & Analytics Associate

Data preparation, workflow design, enrichment, validation, and reporting support.

Tools

Analytics & Automation

Excel, Power BI, Zapier, RocketReach, and CRM data management.

01

Project Overview

Leadership Memphis maintains constituent and organizational contact data used for alumni engagement, fundraising, sponsorship outreach, and reporting.

I worked with staff to improve data quality, reduce repetitive manual work, and create a more reliable foundation for future analytics and outreach.

02

The Challenge

Contact records were distributed across multiple lists and included missing, inconsistent, duplicated, or outdated information.

Preparing those records for outreach required extensive manual review, while original and enriched information sometimes produced different results.

03

What I Did

01

Cleaned and standardized records

Organized source data and corrected inconsistent contact fields across multiple lists.

02

Enriched missing information

Used RocketReach to identify missing professional emails, phone numbers, companies, and job titles.

03

Designed selection rules

Created logic for comparing original and enriched fields and selecting the most reliable value.

04

Tested automated workflows

Built and evaluated Zapier workflows for recurring enrichment and data-processing tasks.

05

Outcome

The project produced a cleaner and more structured contact dataset, established consistent rules for reconciling original and enriched information, and created a more scalable process for future outreach, reporting, and data maintenance.
